Cats On The Wild Side is a small urban cattery located in O'Fallon, Missouri. We have always had a dream to own and work with exotic cats. We were finally able to realize our dream and have been able to mix the best of both worlds. The look and personality of a majestic exotic cat, mixed with a cute cuddly domestic house cat. We will continue to do everything to improve the breeds that we work with and we are committed to continuing to develop the sweet personality which allows these cats to be the perfect pet. Our cats are given the very best of care. From their housing to their nutrition, we work hard to give them the very best quality of care. .
